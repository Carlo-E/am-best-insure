Short Term Plans

Short-term health insurance plans are an excellent option for those who are experiencing a temporary gap in healthcare coverage. These plans are designed for individuals who are between jobs, waiting for employer coverage to start, recent college graduates, or anyone in need of short-term coverage until they can obtain a long-term plan. Short-term health insurance plans provide flexibility and affordability, while offering the peace of mind that comes with knowing you have the coverage you need during times of transition.
